Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This page explains what we store and how we use it.

Minimal Data We store only what is required
No Selling We don’t sell your data
Invite-based Chat works after invite acceptance
1) Information We Collect
We collect only the data needed to run the platform:
  • UID (user identifier) to enable login and linking profiles.
  • Basic profile details (like name, city, subject) provided by the user.
  • Resume details (about, education, skills) if the teacher fills them.
  • Resume PDF if the teacher uploads it (optional unless job apply requires it).
  • Invites & chat messages for communication between teachers and institutes.
2) How We Use Information
We use information only to provide core features:
  • To connect teachers and institutes via browsing and profiles.
  • To enable invite-based communication and accepted chat.
  • To allow job posting, job applications, and application review.
  • To keep the platform safe (for example blocking abusive accounts).
3) Data Sharing
We do not sell or rent your data. We do not share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is shown only within the platform (for example: an institute can view a teacher’s resume after applying or when viewing public profile fields).
4) Security
We use standard practices to protect data, including access controls and safe validation in our APIs. However, no system is 100% secure. Please keep your UID private and avoid sharing sensitive personal information in chats.
5) Your Choices
You control what you submit:
  • You can choose what to put in your profile and resume.
  • You can upload or replace your resume PDF anytime.
  • If you want your data removed, contact us through the platform.
6) Contact
For privacy-related concerns, please contact us through the platform.
